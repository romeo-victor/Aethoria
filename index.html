The Vision

A World That
Remembers

Chronicles of Aethoria is a single-player, story-driven action-RPG set in a continent of five kingdoms, each built on a distinct magical tradition — and all on the brink of war.

You play as Kaelith, marked by a sigil that connects to all five sources of magic in a world where that shouldn't be possible. The game follows the philosophy: "Read → React → Adapt → Master" — in combat, in exploration, and in every relationship you build.

This isn't a game about saving the world from a villain. It's a game about understanding why the villain might be right about the problem — and wrong about the solution.

Our Philosophy

We Don't Need
Perfect Résumés

"We'd rather work with someone who has never shipped a game but stays up at night thinking about how to make one — than someone with ten shipped titles who treats this as just another job."
🔥

Enthusiasm Over Experience

If you're excited about game development and willing to learn, that matters more to us than years on a resume. Skills can be taught. Passion can't.

🤝

Small Team, Big Impact

2–4 people, each with real ownership over their domain. No corporate hierarchy. No ticket systems. Just people making something they believe in.

🌱

Learn As We Build

Nobody on this team knows everything. We figure things out together, share what we learn, and get better with every iteration.

💎

Quality Over Speed

We'd rather build one incredible dungeon than ten mediocre ones. Every system, every line of dialogue, every texture — it has to matter.
